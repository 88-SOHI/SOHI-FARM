About Us

Welcome to Sohi Farm. We are committed to providing fresh, healthy, and high-quality farm products directly to our customers. Our mission is to grow natural products with care and deliver freshness to every home. Welcome to Sohi Farm, a legacy in citrus excellence that began in 1947.
Since our founding, we have been dedicated to cultivating and delivering the finest kinnow oranges from the heart of Pakistan.
With over 75 years of experience, we are proud to have established ourselves as a trusted name, recognized for quality, taste, and freshness.
At Sohi Farm, our journey starts in the rich orchards of Sargodha,
a region known for its fertile soil and ideal climate for growing world-class citrus fruits.
Our kinnows are carefully nurtured and handpicked at the peak of ripeness, ensuring they reach you bursting with flavor and packed with nutrients.
We understand the importance of freshness and quality, which is why our kinnows are meticulously packed to preserve their natural zest and juiciness.
We cater to clients across Pakistan and also serve international markets, bringing a taste of Pakistan’s citrus heritage to the world.,
Every pack of Sohi Farm reflects our commitment to quality, from the farm to your table.,
Thank you for choosing Sohi Farm.
We look forward to brightening your day with the vibrant taste of our signature kinnows!
